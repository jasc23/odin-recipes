Kusinang Pinoy!

Growing up as a Filipino, I have always loved eating "lutong-bahay" or homecooked food. It would be my packed lunch thorughout the course of my elementary years until now that I am an adult.
From homecooked food to street food, I have always been proud of Filipino cuisine. Why? kasi masarap! ( it is delicious!)

There are a lot of Filipino dishes that I would love to share with you, but here are my top 5 that I will never get tired of eating (seriously, they are that good).

Recipe list

  • Adobo
  • Kare-Kare
  • Sinigang
  • Chicharon Bulaklak
  • Puto Bumbong
